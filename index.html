Who I Am

About Me

AR
PhD Researcher

I am a final-year PhD student at IIT Palakkad under the guidance of Dr. Shaikshavali Chitraganti. My work focuses on estimation and control in Networked Control Systems (NCS), with an emphasis on real-world implementation via hardware-in-the-loop systems.

I am passionate about bridging rigorous theoretical foundations with practical engineering — designing algorithms that remain robust in the face of network uncertainties, adversarial disturbances, and real-time constraints.

What I Study

Research Interests

Optimal State Estimation in Unreliable Networks

Designing estimators that remain optimal under packet drops, delays, and intermittent sensor data.

Networked Control & Cyber-Physical Systems

Stability and performance analysis of control loops closed over communication networks.

Robotics & HIL Testing

Hardware-in-the-loop validation platforms for testing estimation and control algorithms on real embedded systems.

Reinforcement Learning in NCS

Adaptive RL-based controllers that learn optimal policies for uncertain and stochastic networked environments.

Security in Feedback Systems

Detection and mitigation of man-in-the-middle attacks, false-data injection, and replay attacks in closed-loop systems.
